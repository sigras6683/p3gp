I love to go on family vacations during the summer, and we always go to a beach in Florida. What's one animal that you always see or hear about in Florida? Manatees! Ever since my first trip to Florida, I have fallen in love with Manatees and want to do as much as I can to help protect and save them, as well as educate others about manatees and how to create a safer enviroment for them.

The Sky

The sky is so beautiful at sunset. Whenever I need to take a break from homework, I look out my window at the sky. Usually the sun is setting by then, turning the sky into a variety of beautiful colors. Depending on the season too the sunset skies range from reds oranges and yellows, to deep ombres of purple.

Especially during the summer, I see kids everywhere in my neighborhood playing outside as it finally begins to cool down, making the outdoors far more enjoyable.
