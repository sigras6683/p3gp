I love to go on family vacations during the summer, and we always go to a beach in Florida. What's one animal that you always see or hear about in Florida? Manatees! Ever since my first trip to Florida, I have fallen in love with Manatees and want to do as much as I can to help protect and save them, as well as educate others about manatees and how to create a safer enviroment for them.
